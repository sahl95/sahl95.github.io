About Me

I am a PhD student in the Astronomy and Astrophysics group at the University of Warwick under the supervision of Dr. Farzana Meru. My research explores the evolution of planets around self-gravitating protoplanetary discs. I perform 3D SPH simulations to investigate the importance of the disc thermodynamics on planet migration. I am also interested in the dust dynamics of self-gravitating discs linking theory with observations by studying the impact of planet migration on the disc structure.
