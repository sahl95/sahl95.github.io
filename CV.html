Research Interests

  • Planet migration in gravitationally unstable protoplanetary discs.
  • Impact of planet migration on the structure of protoplanetary discs.
  • Warped gravitationally unstable protoplanetary discs.
  • The dust dynamics in self-gravitating discs.
  • Connecting simulations with observed disc features.
